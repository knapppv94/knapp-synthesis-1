Mussolini Analysis

Mussolini is considered to be the founding father of fascism, and this is definitely reflected in his word cloud. Note the presence of the word ‘fascist’ near the bottom right of the cloud. Additionally, Mussolini mentions surrounding countries and countries with which Italy was at war with a very high frequency. ‘Comrades’, ‘military’, ‘communal’, ‘intervention’, and ‘struggle’ also appear in this cloud. All of these terms combined help to paint a picture of his original definition of fascism.
